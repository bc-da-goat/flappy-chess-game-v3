About Flappy Chess

Welcome to Flappy Chess!

Flappy Chess is an exciting and unique browser-based game that combines the classic gameplay of Flappy Bird with the strategic world of chess. Navigate your jetpack-powered character through a challenging obstacle course of chess pieces, testing both your reflexes and your knowledge of chess piece movements.

Game Features

  • Unique Gameplay: Experience a fresh take on the endless runner genre with chess-themed obstacles
  • Leaderboard System: Compete with players worldwide and see how you rank on the global leaderboard
  • Customization: Unlock and select from a variety of character skins and backgrounds
  • Coin System: Earn coins based on your score and use them to personalize your gaming experience
  • Mobile Optimized: Play seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices
  • Rank-Based Rewards: Top players get special gold, silver, or bronze skins based on their leaderboard position

How to Play

Use the spacebar (desktop) or tap the screen (mobile) to make your character jump and avoid the chess pieces. Each piece you pass increases your score. The game gets progressively more challenging as you advance!

Tip: Different chess pieces move in different patterns. Learn their movements to better anticipate and avoid them!
